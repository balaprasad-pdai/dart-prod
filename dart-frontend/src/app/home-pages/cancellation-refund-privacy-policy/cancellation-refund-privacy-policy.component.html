CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

  1. Booking Cancellations

    Once a booking is made through the platform, the User may have the option to cancel the booking before the Repair Order is processed. After the date of Repair Order the customer will no longer have an option to cancel the booking.

    In case the booking is cancelled before the Repair Order is processed, the relevant amount paid if any will be refunded within 14 working days (from the day of cancellation), and the amount shall be credited or transferred to the bank account through which the payment was made.

    For any other issues relating to booking cancellations or refunds please contact the relevant DART location directly.

All or any other specific policies regarding our services shall be made available on the FAQ tab of the Platform, all of which are incorporated here by reference.
